## Risk factors
- Previous #: is the single highest predictor
- Back pain
- Smoking
- Low weight
- Menopause age
- Hypogonadism
- Vitamin D deficiency
- Calcium intake
- Steroids
- Alcohol
- Fall risk
- **Diabetes Mellitus**

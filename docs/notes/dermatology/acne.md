## Acne

Source: CME talk by Dr Alexander Wong Shing Yip | Beacon medical group

> Acne requires long term treatment: need to counsel patient

## 2 Main mechanisms

- Occlusion of pores
- Infection-inflammation cycle: cutibacterium acne

Consider these mechanisms in the treatment and what the drugs target.

## Treatment options

### Benzoylperoxide (BP) : Good basic and good for body odours

Some of the preparations are hard to find, and have to contact the compounding pharmacies.

### Hormonal acne

- Worse during menses
- "All acne are hormonal"
- OCP, Spironolactone (off label)
- Winlevi (Clascoterone) --> inhibit sebum production, ↓ C. Acne, ↓ Hyperkeratinisation

### PO Isotretinoin

## Dr Alex personal formula

- Clindamycin topical short course
- Face wash: chlorhexidine: for cost
- Topical tretinoin
- Winlevi: BD 3/12 (if >12 y/o), usually 1 tube/month, avoid direct sun/heat exposure to tube
